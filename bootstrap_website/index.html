About Us

We are a rescue center in Metrowest Boston aiming to find homes for abandoned, stray, and traumatized dogs. What makes us unique is that we want the journey to a forever home to be as warm, welcoming, and comfortable as the destination. Check out our available dogs and come in to meet them to find which pup would fit best with your family!
